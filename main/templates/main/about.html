Our Goal

Key Of Success

powering a transparent recruitment platform for all kinds of jobs with the power of blockchain technology.

What is DARA

DARA is a Decentralized AI-based Requitment Agency Platform.

DARA wishes to decentralize the typical recruitment paradigm where recruiters, job seekers and recruitment agencies are united thereby providing more value for the future of recruitment, whether or not you are a recruitment agency, a job seeker wishing to work for a toptier agency or a company seeking qualified and talented workers DARA ensures you achieve your desire.
there exists millions of skilled job seekers and companies needing their services but often due to the lack of information and improper recruitment proceedings companies end up hiring the wrong candidates and the skilled ones a left hanging. DARA provides the perfect solution by creating a decentralized platform where job seekers get real time information where ever they are and recruitment agencies are provided with AI mechanism to aid them during interviews.
